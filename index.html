Who am I?

I am currently a grad student at Carnegie Mellon University focussing on large-scale distributed software architectures. Previously, I worked as a Software Engineer at Amazon Web Services on distributed data stores and also worked on AR cloud infrastructures for NASA’s Artemis mission with the NASA S.U.I.T.S. program. Outside work, I spend my time biking, taking long naps, and teaching algorithms on YouTube.
